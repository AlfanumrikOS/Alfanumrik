Bilkul sahi pakda. Tumne factory ke raw materials bata diye. Ab dekho — yeh raha woh poori equation jo NCERT chapter 1 mein likhi hai:

Equation · NCERT Cl 8 · Ch 1 
      sunlight
        ↓
6 CO₂  +  6 H₂O  ────────►  C₆H₁₂O₆  +  6 O₂
   │        │      chlorophyll        │         │
   │        │                         │         │
 carbon   water                    glucose    oxygen
 dioxide                          (food!)   (we breathe)
Plant ke leaves "carbon + water + light" ko "sugar + oxygen" mein convert karte hain — yeh sirf 1 cycle hai, but inside ye 2 stages mein hota hai.

Ab tumhare original sawaal pe — light reaction aur dark reaction waali confusion. Naam dhokha de raha hai. Yeh dono actually 2 alag-alag steps hain ek hi factory ke andar:

step 01
light
Sunlight chlorophyll ko hit karta hai. Pani (H₂O) tootta hai — yahaan se oxygen nikalta hai (jo hum saans lete hain). Ye step sirf din mein hota hai — kyunki sunlight chahiye.
step 02
dark
Step 1 mein bani energy ka use karke, CO₂ ko glucose mein convert kiya jaata hai. "Dark" naam isliye padaa kyunki direct sunlight ki zaroorat nahi — but ye din mein bhi ho sakta hai.

Ek line mein — light reaction ingredients tod-ti hai, dark reaction khaana banaati hai. Samjha?

Sahi answer choose karo: Photolysis kis step mein hota hai?
Sahi! Photolysis = "photo" (light) + "lysis" (breaking). Yeh light reaction mein hi hota hai, kyunki sunlight ki zaroorat hoti hai paani todne ke liye. Yahin se O₂ release hota hai jo plant baahar chhodta hai.

Foxy·7:48 pm

Ek baar quickly recap karte hain jo abhi seekha:

recap 01
Photosynthesis = leaf factory mein CO₂ + H₂O se glucose + O₂ banana.
recap 02
Light reaction → photolysis → O₂ release.
recap 03
Dark / Calvin cycle → CO₂ + energy → glucose. "Dark" = light optional.

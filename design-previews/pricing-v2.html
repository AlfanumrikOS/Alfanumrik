Questions, answered.

Section iv · the small print, in plain words
i.
What if my child doesn't take to it — can I get a refund?
Yes. Every paid plan comes with a 7-day full refund, no questions asked. Email support@alfanumrik.com within seven days of payment and we will reverse the charge to your original payment method within 5—7 working days. No screenshots, no exit interviews.
+
ii.
How does the billing cycle work — and when am I charged?
Monthly plans are billed via Razorpay every 30 days from your signup date — recurring auto-debit on UPI, cards, or netbanking. Yearly plans are a single upfront charge for 365 days; we email you a reminder 14 days before renewal. Cancel anytime from your dashboard, and you keep access until the period you have already paid for ends.
+
iii.
Can I switch between plans — say, Starter to Pro mid-month?
Yes, switch up or down anytime from your account page. Upgrades take effect immediately and we pro-rate the price for the days remaining in your cycle. Downgrades take effect at the next billing date — you keep the higher plan's features until then. There is no fee for switching.
+
iv.
Is GST included in these prices, or extra?
All prices shown are inclusive of 18% GST. The price you see is the price you pay. Your invoice (downloadable from your account) will show the base amount and GST broken out separately for your records — useful if you need it for school reimbursement or your own books.
+
v.
Which payment methods do you accept?
Everything Razorpay supports for Indian customers — UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, Amex), netbanking from 50+ banks, and wallets. UPI auto-pay works on monthly subscriptions. We do not store your card details — Razorpay tokenises everything to RBI standards.
+
vi.
I have three children — is Family really cheaper than three Pro plans?
Yes, considerably. Three Pro plans would cost ₹2,097/month or ₹16,797/year. Family is ₹999/month or ₹7,999/year for the same unlimited features across all three children. You save roughly ₹1,100 every month — about ₹13,000 a year — and you get one bill, one dashboard, one PTM-ready summary across all three.
+

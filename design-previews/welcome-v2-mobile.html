Not a chatbot. A tutor.

Stop 01 · Foxy session

Foxy, who never sighs.

She asks back. She draws diagrams. She says "shall we try a smaller example?" instead of "wrong, try again."

A

F
Good question, Aarav. shall we draw it together? Light is the energy that lets the plant build sugar from CO₂ and water…
A
So leaves are the kitchen?
F
exactly — and chlorophyll is the cook.

Wrong? Foxy re-asks.

Get a question wrong, and the misconception is logged. Three days later, the same idea comes back — phrased differently. That's how things stick.

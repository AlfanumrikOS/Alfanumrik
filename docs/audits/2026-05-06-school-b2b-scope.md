## Headline

The school B2B surface is **mature, not half-built**. Earlier framing (from Phase 0) of "school portal half-finished" was wrong. Real numbers: 17 school-admin API routes (5,054 LOC), 16 school-admin UI pages including a 39 KB setup wizard, 6 onboarding routes (1,168 LOC), full RBAC delegation/elevation/approval system, OAuth (519 LOC), monthly-billing cron with `school_invoices` + Razorpay invoice IDs, WhatsApp notifications (en/hi templates, E.164 validated), and shipped multi-tenant RLS scoping every relevant table by `school_id`.

What blocks selling to a paid school today is small and concrete: school admins **cannot self-serve a plan change or payment** through their own portal. The subscription route is GET-only.

## Headline blockers to a paid school sale today

1. **No self-service subscription purchase from school-admin.** `subscription/route.ts` is GET-only. A school admin cannot upgrade plan, increase seats, or trigger a Razorpay subscription on their own. They must call sales / super-admin. This is the single biggest blocker to scaled B2B sales.
2. **No school-admin SSO/OAuth-client self-service.** The `oauth/authorize` and `oauth/token` endpoints are real, but creating a school-specific OAuth client app requires super-admin (`super-admin/oauth-apps`). Schools that want to integrate their existing IdP cannot do it themselves.
3. **WhatsApp template set is student-centric.** The 4 templates are all student notifications. There's no parent-facing weekly progress digest template, no school-admin alert template (e.g., "10 students scored below 40% this week"), and no teacher template. School pilots typically want parent-facing comms wired before signing.
4. **Invoice flow lacks a "send to school" path.** `school_invoices` are generated by cron and listed in the API, but I don't see a route that emails the invoice PDF to the school admin or marks invoices `sent`. Without this, every billing month requires a manual operator step.
5. **Trial → paid conversion path is unclear.** `schools/trial/route.ts` exists at 272 LOC; needs paired UI review to confirm trial expiry warnings and conversion CTAs are present in `school-admin/billing`.

## Surprisingly mature (don't redo)

- **Full RBAC delegation system.** `requestApproval`, `approveRequest`, `grantElevation`, `createDelegationToken` — production-grade granular permission system. Far beyond what most early-stage SaaS ships.
- **Audit logging** on every school-admin mutation via `logAudit`.
- **White-label branding API** (`school-admin/branding`).
- **API keys for schools**, with revoke + rotation hooks.
- **Multi-tenant RLS** is correctly enforced at the DB layer — not a "soft" tenant boundary.
- **Onboarding wizard is real** — 28 KB enroll page, 39 KB setup page. School can self-onboard up to the point of paying.
- **Bilingual WhatsApp** templates (en + hi) shipped.
- **Monthly invoice cron** generates `school_invoices` with Razorpay invoice IDs already wired.

## Implications for Phase 2

The "school B2B is half-built" thesis is incorrect. The single biggest revenue-blocking gap is **self-service subscription purchase + plan change in school-admin**. A focused Phase 2 sub-project to ship that single capability — POST/PUT on `school-admin/subscription` route, paired UI in `school-admin/billing`, Razorpay subscription flow scoped to the school, seat-count enforcement — would likely unblock paid pilots without touching anything else.

Total estimated effort for that single fix: ~1 week solo. Compare to 2-4 weeks for "build school portal" which was the assumption going in.

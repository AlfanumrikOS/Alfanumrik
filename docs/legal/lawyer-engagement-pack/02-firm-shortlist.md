# Firm Shortlist

Seven Indian law firms with the practice combination Alfanumrik needs: data protection (DPDP Act + IT Act), tech / IT, consumer protection, payments / RBI, and minors / education sector.

This is a descriptive shortlist, not a recommendation. Selection depends on budget, response speed, and direct fit with the founder's working style. All contacts listed are public-facing channels (firm websites and `info@`-style addresses); individual lawyers' personal contacts are intentionally omitted.

Tiers reflect typical engagement cost only; tier does not imply quality.

---

## Tier 1 — Startup / mid-market friendly

### 1. Ikigai Law

- **Practice areas relevant here:** data protection (DPDP Act), TMT (technology, media, telecom), fintech & payments (RBI), platform regulation, AI & emerging tech, gaming / EdTech overlap.
- **Engagement model:** project-based for policy work; retainer for ongoing advisory. Frequently quotes fixed-fee for Privacy Policy + ToS reviews.
- **Approximate cost band:** INR 1.5L - 4L for a Privacy + ToS review pass for an early-stage product.
- **Why they fit Alfanumrik:** Ikigai has published extensively on DPDP Act implementation, online platform liability, and minors-on-platforms questions. Their TMT practice regularly handles consumer-tech subscriptions and Razorpay-style payment flows.
- **Public contact:** https://www.ikigailaw.com — `contact@ikigailaw.com`
- **Approximate cold-outreach response time:** 2-5 business days.

### 2. Spice Route Legal

- **Practice areas relevant here:** technology contracts, data privacy, fintech & payments, consumer-facing digital services, regulatory advisory.
- **Engagement model:** project-based for one-off reviews; monthly retainer common for early-stage consumer-tech.
- **Approximate cost band:** INR 1L - 3L for a Privacy + ToS review.
- **Why they fit Alfanumrik:** Spice Route is known for working with seed-to-Series-B startups in regulated consumer verticals. Their tech-contracts practice covers SaaS terms, subscription billing, and India-localized data flows.
- **Public contact:** https://spicerouttelegal.com — `info@spicerouttelegal.com`
- **Approximate cold-outreach response time:** 2-4 business days.

### 3. TechLegis

- **Practice areas relevant here:** information technology, data protection, intellectual property, digital media regulation, e-commerce.
- **Engagement model:** project-based and hourly; willing to scope tightly.
- **Approximate cost band:** INR 80K - 2.5L for a Privacy + ToS review.
- **Why they fit Alfanumrik:** focused tech-and-IP boutique with documented work on IT Rules 2021 compliance for digital platforms. Reasonable fit for a single-product consumer review.
- **Public contact:** https://techlegis.com — `info@techlegis.com`
- **Approximate cold-outreach response time:** 3-7 business days.

## Tier 2 — Premium full-service

### 4. Nishith Desai Associates (NDA)

- **Practice areas relevant here:** technology law, data protection & privacy, fintech / payments / RBI, education sector, intellectual property, dispute resolution.
- **Engagement model:** typically project-based with senior-partner involvement; retainer also available.
- **Approximate cost band:** INR 5L - 15L+ for a Privacy + ToS review with full memos. Premium-tier rates.
- **Why they fit Alfanumrik:** NDA has a dedicated education-sector practice and has published research papers on EdTech regulation in India, child protection online, and the DPDP Act. Strong fit for K-12-specific questions on minors' contracts and parental consent. Their fintech bench can review the RBI e-mandate clauses.
- **Public contact:** https://www.nishithdesai.com — `nda@nishithdesai.com`
- **Approximate cold-outreach response time:** 3-7 business days; intake call typically scheduled within 2 weeks.

### 5. Trilegal

- **Practice areas relevant here:** technology, media & telecommunications; data protection & privacy; fintech / payments; consumer protection; dispute resolution.
- **Engagement model:** project-based for defined scope; retainer for ongoing advisory.
- **Approximate cost band:** INR 5L - 12L for a Privacy + ToS review.
- **Why they fit Alfanumrik:** large TMT and data-protection practice with depth on the DPDP Act, IT Rules 2021, and platform-liability issues. Trilegal regularly advises consumer-internet platforms on subscription terms and Indian payment regulations.
- **Public contact:** https://www.trilegal.com — `info@trilegal.com`
- **Approximate cold-outreach response time:** 3-7 business days.

### 6. AZB & Partners

- **Practice areas relevant here:** technology, media & telecom; data privacy; banking & finance / RBI / payments; consumer protection; intellectual property.
- **Engagement model:** project-based; retainer also available.
- **Approximate cost band:** INR 6L - 15L+ for a Privacy + ToS review. Premium-tier rates.
- **Why they fit Alfanumrik:** broad commercial practice with strong fintech / payments depth. Fit if the founder anticipates needing the same firm for institutional / school B2B contracts, fundraise documentation, and dispute work later. Less specialised in EdTech-for-minors than NDA.
- **Public contact:** https://www.azbpartners.com — `mumbai@azbpartners.com` (or the city-specific intake address on the website)
- **Approximate cold-outreach response time:** 5-10 business days.

### 7. Khaitan & Co

- **Practice areas relevant here:** technology, media & telecommunications; data protection; banking & finance; corporate / commercial; intellectual property; dispute resolution.
- **Engagement model:** project-based; retainer also available.
- **Approximate cost band:** INR 5L - 12L for a Privacy + ToS review.
- **Why they fit Alfanumrik:** general-corporate full-service firm with reliable TMT and data-protection benches. Less EdTech-specialised than NDA but a safe choice if the founder wants a single firm for both regulatory and corporate needs.
- **Public contact:** https://www.khaitanco.com — intake form on the website; general queries via `info@khaitanco.com`
- **Approximate cold-outreach response time:** 5-10 business days.

## Selection guide

| If you want... | Consider |
|---|---|
| Lowest cost, fast turn-around, single-product scope | Tier 1 (Ikigai, Spice Route, TechLegis) |
| Deep K-12 EdTech / minors / education-sector experience | NDA (Tier 2) |
| Future-proof for fundraise + institutional contracts under one firm | AZB or Khaitan & Co (Tier 2) |
| Strongest payments / RBI bench in addition to data | Trilegal or AZB (Tier 2) |
| Public DPDP Act thought leadership | Ikigai (Tier 1) or NDA (Tier 2) |

## Notes

- All cost bands are approximate, based on publicly observable engagement patterns for early-stage consumer-tech work in India in 2025-2026. Actual quotes will vary by scope, urgency, and partner involvement.
- Cold-outreach response times are anecdotal averages; individual responses can be faster or slower.
- This list is not exhaustive. Other competent options exist (Cyril Amarchand Mangaldas, J. Sagar Associates, Saraf & Partners, Krishnamurthy & Co, etc.) but the seven above cover the practice combinations Alfanumrik needs at distinct price points.
- Engaging two firms (e.g., one for Privacy + ToS, a different one later for fundraise documents) is common and not a problem; partnership economics differ from pricing-page rates.
